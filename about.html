About the human behind the bear

Hi, I’m the keeper of this tiny den on the internet. I believe in calm, handcrafted websites and writing that gives you time to breathe. This template was put together so I could share notes on design, hiking, and intentional technology—but you can adapt it to whatever you care about most.

Swap this text for your own story. Let readers know what you’re exploring, the projects you’re proud of, and where they can find you elsewhere on the web. If you want a contact section, add a simple list of links or an email button below.

Because the site is static, there’s no database, tracking scripts, or superfluous animation. Just words, photos, and a little CSS. That means your visitors get the content immediately without distractions.
