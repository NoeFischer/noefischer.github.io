Slow Internet, Soft Mind

May 18, 2024

When everything on the web is tuned for speed, it can feel strange to intentionally slow down. Yet the quiet corners may be where your best ideas live—far from autoplay videos, algorithmic feeds, and inbox badges. A slower internet makes space for the kind of wandering that writing craves.

The philosophy behind Bear Blog is to default to less: fewer colors, fewer scripts, fewer distractions. Every byte you remove is an invitation to concentrate. The page you are reading now is pure HTML and CSS served from a static host. That simplicity keeps the lighthouse score perfect and the DOM easy to understand.

“The secret to creativity is subtracting the noise until the signal feels inevitable.”

Try it for yourself: pause notifications, swap your phone for a notebook, and draft your next post offline. When you come back, publishing is as easy as copying a file into the posts/ folder and linking to it from the index. The workflow rewards focus instead of frantic multitasking.

May your internet be intentionally quiet, and your mind pleasantly soft.
